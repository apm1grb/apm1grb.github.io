    Class PaneDeviceManager<T>

    A PaneDeviceManager<T> is used to manage Device<T>s which are not handled as a separate node in the DeviceTree. Instead they are handled at the right side of the ConfigClient by selecting their belonging DeviceType node in the DeviceTree (e.g. enterprise Server).
